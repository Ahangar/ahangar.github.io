About me

I am an air quality specialist at South Coast Air Quality Management District primarily working on developing and implementing monitoring plans for environmental justice communities. My job includes data analysis, data visualization, physical modeling and result communication.
I received my Ph.D. in mechanical engineering focusing on air quality and atmospheric modelling and worked on various projects related to the power grid, emission modelling, CAD modelling, and astronomy.

Projects

Interactive Dashboard for Communicating Air Quality Monitoring Results (2020-present)

In this project at South Coast AQMD, interactive dashboards and reports were created using ArcGIS, R Shiny, and Tableau to communicate air monitoring results in environmental justice communities (See the Dashboards).

Climate Impact of Manure Management from California Dairies (2018-2019)

The goal of this project, funded by the University of California Office of the President (UCOP), was to measure, model, and estimate the emissions of GHGs and other air pollutants from dairies using a multi-tiered approach including field sampling, aerial measurements, air pollution dispersion modeling, and process-based modeling. (Published Paper).

Satellite-Derived PM2.5 Grids with Dispersion Model Downscaling: A Method to Generate Continuous Long-Term Fine Particulate Data for Community-Scale Air Quality Health Research (2017-2018)

In this project funded by NASA Earth Science Division Applied Sciences Program, air quality dispersion models were used to downscale sattelite or stationary measurements into local scale concentration maps (Published Paper).

Effectiveness of Sound Wall-Vegetation Combination Barriers as Near-Roadway Pollutant Mitigation Strategies (2014-2017)

This project, funded by California Air Resources Board (CARB), examined the eﬀects of road conﬁgurations and urban vegetation on the air quality impact of vehicle-related emissions and used this understanding to suggest methods to mitigate the impact of these emissions on urban air quality (Final Report).
